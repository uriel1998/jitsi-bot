# Logistics and Supply Procedures

Managed by **June Calder** under Field Operations.

## Checkout Rules
- Submit gear request 24 hours before mission.
- Sign item condition at pickup and return.
- Report damage within 2 hours of return.

## Standard Kit
- First-aid pack
- Thermal camera
- GPS beacon
- Sample containers
- Weather radio

## Vehicle Policy
- Two-driver rule for trips over 90 minutes.
- Fuel log updated after every mission.

Missing gear is escalated from Team Lead to Logistics Manager, then Operations Captain.
