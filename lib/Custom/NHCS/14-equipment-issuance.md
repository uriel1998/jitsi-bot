# Equipment Issuance Handout

Issued gear remains NHCS property.

## Default Issue
- Field jacket
- Radio unit
- ID badge
- Notebook kit

## Member Responsibilities
- Keep gear clean and functional.
- Do not lend gear outside NHCS.
- Report malfunction immediately.

## Replacement Policy
- Normal wear: no fee.
- Negligence loss: replacement contribution required.

## Return Triggers
Gear must be returned within 72 hours after resignation, removal, or extended leave.
