# Mission and Values

The **North Hollow Cryptid Society (NHCS)** protects local ecosystems and communities by documenting unusual wildlife reports responsibly.

## Mission
- Investigate credible cryptid sightings.
- Preserve evidence integrity.
- Educate the public without creating panic.

## Core Values
- **Safety First:** No evidence is worth injury.
- **Scientific Rigor:** Log before you speculate.
- **Community Trust:** Report findings honestly.
- **Stewardship:** Leave habitats better than we found them.

## Motto
"Observe. Record. Respect."
