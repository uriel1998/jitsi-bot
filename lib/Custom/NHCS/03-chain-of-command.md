# Chain of Command

Use this reporting path for all decisions:

1. **Scout or Specialist**
2. **Team Lead**
3. **Operations Captain Talia Reed**
4. **Deputy Director Owen Pike**
5. **Director Mara Quill**

## Emergency Exception
If immediate danger exists, contact the nearest authority first, then notify your Team Lead.

## Escalation Rules
- Safety incidents: within 15 minutes.
- Evidence disputes: within 4 hours.
- Media inquiries: immediately to Petra Snow.

Skipping levels is allowed only for safety, legal, or ethics concerns.
