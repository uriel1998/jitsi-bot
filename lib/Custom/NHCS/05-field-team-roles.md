# Field Team Roles

Each mission team has four roles.

## Team Lead
- Final on-site authority.
- Reports to Operations Captain.

## Tracker
- Reads trails and habitat signs.
- Advises route and pacing.

## Tech Specialist
- Operates cameras, drones, and sensors.
- Maintains timestamped logs.

## Safety Medic
- Conducts health checks.
- Controls evacuation decisions.

## Minimum Staffing
No mission launches with fewer than three certified members.

## Shift Rule
Night operations require two rested alternates.
