# Safety Handbook Summary

## Non-Negotiable Rules
- Never operate alone.
- Carry charged radio and backup battery.
- Mark entry and exit routes before dusk.
- End mission at first severe weather alert.

## Hazard Codes
- **Code Amber:** uncertain terrain risk.
- **Code Red:** immediate evacuation.

## Medical Reporting
All injuries, including minor strains, must be logged before shift end.

## Command Authority
During safety events, **Safety Medic** can overrule Team Lead decisions until conditions stabilize.
