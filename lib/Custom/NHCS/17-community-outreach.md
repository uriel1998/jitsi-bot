# Community Outreach Plan

NHCS builds trust through local education and transparency.

## Outreach Programs
- Monthly town hall Q&A
- School wildlife awareness talks
- Seasonal trail safety workshops

## Responsibilities
- **Petra Snow** schedules events.
- Field volunteers provide demonstrations.
- Research team prepares fact sheets.

## Success Metrics
- Attendance totals
- Follow-up volunteer signups
- Reduction in false alarm calls

Outreach summaries are presented quarterly to leadership.
