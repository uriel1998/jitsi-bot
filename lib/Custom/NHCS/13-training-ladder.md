# Training Ladder

Progression is performance-based, not tenure-based.

## Levels
- **Initiate:** orientation complete.
- **Scout:** basic field certified.
- **Senior Scout:** leads patrol segments.
- **Specialist:** advanced track (tracking, tech, medical).
- **Team Lead:** mission command certified.

## Promotion Requirements
- Current level for at least 3 months
- Two positive evaluations
- No unresolved safety violations
- Skills test pass

Promotions are recommended by Team Leads and approved by **Talia Reed**.
