# Evidence Handling Protocol

Evidence must be traceable from collection to archive.

## Collection Steps
1. Photograph item in place with scale marker.
2. Record GPS, time, weather, and collector name.
3. Seal in labeled container.
4. Submit chain-of-custody form.

## Storage
- Biological samples: refrigerated locker B.
- Plaster casts: dry shelf C.
- Audio/video: encrypted archive server.

## Approval Flow
Field collector -> Team Lead -> Research intake clerk -> Dr. Vale.

Any broken chain-of-custody marks evidence as non-validated.
