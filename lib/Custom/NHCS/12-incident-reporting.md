# Incident Reporting Process

Use this process for injuries, conflicts, lost gear, or public complaints.

## Deadlines
- Critical incidents: submit within 1 hour.
- Non-critical incidents: submit by end of day.

## Required Fields
- Date and time
- Location
- People involved
- Immediate actions taken
- Recommended prevention step

## Review Path
Team Lead reviews first, then sends to **Talia Reed**.
Serious incidents are escalated to **Mara Quill** and legal advisor.

No retaliation is permitted for good-faith reporting.
