# Research and Analysis Division

Research converts field observations into defensible findings.

## Responsibilities
- Verify metadata for photos, audio, and casts.
- Compare sightings against known species behavior.
- Maintain case database and confidence ratings.

## Reporting Flow
Analysts report to **Dr. Ishan Vale**.
Dr. Vale submits weekly summaries to **Owen Pike**.

## Confidence Scale
- **A:** Multiple independent evidence types.
- **B:** Strong single-source evidence.
- **C:** Incomplete but plausible.
- **D:** Likely misidentification.

Only A and B cases are used in public statements.
