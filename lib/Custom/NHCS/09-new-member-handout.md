# New Member Handout

Welcome to NHCS.

## First Week Checklist
- Read Safety Manual and Code of Conduct.
- Meet your Team Lead.
- Complete radio protocol drill.
- Set up your mission log account.

## Your Supervisor
Your direct supervisor is your assigned **Team Lead**. Questions unresolved in 48 hours go to **Talia Reed**.

## What to Bring
- Durable boots
- Weather layers
- Headlamp
- Refillable water bottle

## First 30 Days Goal
Shadow two missions and submit one complete observation log.
