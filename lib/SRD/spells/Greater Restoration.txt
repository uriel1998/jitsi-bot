

Greater Restoration
-------------------

*5th-level abjuration*

**Casting Time:** 1 action

**Range:** Touch

**Components:** V, S, M (diamond dust worth at least 100 gp, which the
spell consumes)

**Duration:** Instantaneous


   single: exhaustion; cured by Greater Restoration
   single: charmed; cured by Greater Restoration
   single: petrified; cured by Greater Restoration
   single: cursed; cured by Greater Restoration
   single: ability score reduction; cured by Greater Restoration
   single: hit point maximum reduction; cured by Greater Restoration

You imbue a creature you touch with positive energy to undo a
debilitating effect. You can reduce the target's `exhaustion <exhaustion>` level by
one, or end one of the following effects on the target:

-  One effect that `charmed` or `petrified` the target

-  One curse, including the target's attunement to a cursed magic item

-  Any reduction to one of the target's ability scores

-  One effect reducing the target's hit point maximum
