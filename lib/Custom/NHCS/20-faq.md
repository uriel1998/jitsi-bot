# Member FAQ

## Are we law enforcement?
No. NHCS documents wildlife anomalies and coordinates with authorities when necessary.

## Can I post mission photos?
Only after Research clears them and witness privacy is protected.

## Who approves mission plans?
Team Leads draft plans, and **Talia Reed** approves launch readiness.

## How do I request training?
Submit a training request form to your Team Lead during weekly check-in.

## What if I disagree with a report?
File an evidence review request with Research within 7 days of report publication.
