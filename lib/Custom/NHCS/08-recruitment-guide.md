# Recruitment Guide

NHCS recruits from local outdoor clubs, colleges, and emergency response groups.

## Basic Requirements
- Age 21+
- Background check clearance
- Wilderness first-aid certification
- Two references

## Application Steps
1. Submit online form and interest statement.
2. Attend group orientation.
3. Complete skills assessment hike.
4. Interview with unit supervisor.

## Selection Priorities
- Calm decision-making
- Evidence discipline
- Team communication

Final selection is approved by **Owen Pike** and recorded by HR volunteer staff.
